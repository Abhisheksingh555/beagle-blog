The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling. With a great sense of smell and tracking instincts, Beagles are widely used as detection dogs. Their friendly nature and compact size make them ideal pets.

Trivia

  1. Height: 13-16 in (33-41 cm)
  2. Weight: 22-25 lb (10.0-11.3 kg)
  3. Coat: Short-haired, medium-length
  4. Color: Tricolor or white with black & tan/brown
  5. Life span: 12-15 years
